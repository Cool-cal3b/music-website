About

CoolestCal3b is a multi-genre producer and song-writer from the small town of Ridgecrest in Southern California. His music can be described as new-age ambient electronic music. He has slowly been gathering a strong following and his popularity is steadily growing

Having started producing music in just 9th grade after downloading FL Studio, Caleb has had many years of culturing his unique sound. His biggest inspirations are Coldplay, EDEN, Imagine Dragons, and Kev Dawg G.
